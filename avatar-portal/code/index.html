👁️ What I See
Detects emotions from your voice in real-time and adjusts NoVo's responses to be more empathic

📖 Button Guide

🎤 Start Talking: Begin voice conversation with NoVo. Speak naturally and she'll respond with voice and emotion.
🛑 Stop: End the current conversation. NoVo will stop listening and speaking.
🔄 Reset (Top-Left): Clear all conversation history and reload the app for a fresh start.
📷 Camera: Turn camera on/off. When on, NoVo can see you and analyze what you show her.
⚙️ Settings: Toggle this admin panel to change AI model, voice, and microphone.
📸 Photo Feature: Say "take my photo" → NoVo responds → Say "shoot" → 3-2-1 countdown → Photo captured! NoVo will then describe what she sees.
😊 Emotion Buttons: Small colored dots show NoVo's current emotion detected from your voice tone (Happy, Sad, Angry, Thinking, Suspicious, Neutral).
